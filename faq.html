FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does a loan with eLending work?

eLending loans are a quick and easy way to borrow money without a credit check or hassle. Loans are based on the value of your collateral, not your credit rating or pay schedule. We have three ways you can obtain capital:
1: A Term Loan: In which we extend a loan to you against your collateral and you have a longer term to pay it off. This loan typically has a higher interest rate than the bridge loan
2: A Bridge Loan: A loan against the value of your luxury asset paid off in a shorter period of time
3: A Sales Advance: We advance you a portion of the value of your loan while we attempt to sell it for the best possible price.

Will I lose my merchandise?

Your assets are completely secure with us. As long as you repay your debt and interest you can retrieve your merchandise. If you decide not to repay you forfeit the collateral.

How do you determine an item’s worth?

We base loan amounts on the value of your item—its current appraised value, its current condition and our ability to sell the item. We use all the research tools we have at our disposal to determine an item’s value and get you the most money we can.

The appraisal process varies depending on the type of item—for example, jewelry is evaluated differently than art. We research and review items to ensure that our loan values are in line with the general marketplace. To obtain the item’s retail value when it was new, its pre-owned market value, and how to determine its condition.

How do you determine the condition of an item?

The condition of an item is always taken into account during the appraisal process of a loan. If one High End Purse comes in that is in perfect condition but no original packaging and one identical purse that has original packaging but with slight wear and tear, we will likely lend more on the first one.

How do you determine the value of jewelry?

When jewelry is the collateral for a loan, we test the precious metals and diamonds to determine its worth. The diamond testing includes a visual inspection,, weighing the item, sizing (determining carat weight) any stones, and noting the cut/color/clarity of each—you may know this as the 4 Cs.

In addition, we test the metal using a special acid that determines the karat of gold. This is done to ensure a clean test so there are no false readings on your item that may detract from what we can loan you.

Is my collateral safe while in eLending’s care?

While your item is with eLending, you still own it. It is our responsibility to keep it safe and in good condition while it’s in our care. Your item is safely stored here with us and is fully insured for the loan value of the collateral we keep.
