About eLending

eLending allows you unleash the power of your equity discreetly and quickly. If you own luxury assets, why not have it work for you and provide you with liquidity. If you have an immediate opportunity or you need to mitigate an unplanned situation, why not tap the value of your illiquid assets with the help of eLending.

eLending offers several ways to realize the value from your assets:

With our SALES LOAN OPTION eCapital will advance a portion of your asset’s value immediately and then maximize the sales value fast. Our interests our aligned because we want to get the best possible value for you so that we can get a higher commission for our efforts.

Don’t want to sell your asset? With our BRIDGE LOAN program eCapital will lend you money against your asset and your asset is returned to you at the repayment of the loan. Your assets are kept safe and insured.

Some understand the amazing potential of our TERM LOAN in which we will advance a minimum of $25,000 at a competitive monthly interest secured against your assets.

All of our assets backed lending platforms are fast, simple, and competitive but most of all discreet.

Traditional banks cannot offer you what eLending does. We transfer funds quickly and protect your assets the same way a museum or auction house does.
